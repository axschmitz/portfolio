I am a Game Developer based in Oberhausen, Germany. Experienced with Unreal Engine and Unity. Probably the fastest learner of new technologies that you can find.

About Me

Since my early childhood, I loved playing video games. From strategy games like Emperor: Rise of the middle Kingdom to shooter games like Counter Strike, I played them all. Video Games are my passion and once I discovered how they are made, I instantly got hooked. From 2018 - 2023, I studied Media Computer Science at the University of applied Sciences in Gelsenkirchen. Aside from my studies, I discovered Unity and started developing my first small game with it. Later university projects introduced me to Unreal Engine and Blueprints. Because I prefer programming over visual scripting, I started to use C++ more and more. I fell so much in love with Unreal, that I wrote my bachelor's thesis about the differences between C++ and Blueprints in Unreal Engine 5. Now I am using it to develop a multiplayer first-person shooter game called Highlander.

Projects

Highlander

Highlander is a multiplayer first-person shooter. It is being developed with Unreal Engine 5. It uses listen-servers to connects players via the steam-subsystem. Right now it features 8 different Weapons, including projectile weapons like rocket- or grenadelaunchers but also hit scan weapons like shotguns or smgs.
